Heya! Lets go over the rules of the game before we get started!

Passwords:

since Killbot doesn't know Javascript she can't save your progress, and has to use passwords instead. They come in a few forms.

Save Points:

At the beginning of each area in the game you'll be given a save password that can bring you back to the beginning of the area! To use them enter them into the address bar after killbot.gay/

Items:

Sometimes in your travels you'll find an item! When you find an item you will be given a 4 letter code. Hold onto codes, sometimes you'll find a spot to enter them to use the item! Most items are not required to progress, but may allow you to skip areas, unlock extra scenes, or uncover fun secrets!

Secrets:

There's also plenty of secret passwords hidden around the site, if you think you've found one use it the same as a save password to uncover some fun hidden stuff!
